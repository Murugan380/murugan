About :

Amazon Salon offers a range of hair and beauty services, including haircuts, styling, coloring, and makeup application. They also incorporate technology, allowing customers to virtually try on different hair colors and styles using augmented reality. Additionally, they offer a retail space where customers can purchase beauty products and tools.

Hair Services:
Amazon Salon provides a full range of hairdressing services for adults and children, including cuts, blow dries, highlights, balayage, and various styling options. Technology Integration: A key feature of Amazon Salon is its use of technology. Customers can experiment with different hair colors using augmented reality (AR) technology.
